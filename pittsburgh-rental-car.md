## Mandatory Fee & Tax Structure at PIT

All airport car rentals at Pittsburgh International carry these overhead charges on top of the advertised base rate:

| Fee Component | Rate |
|---|---|
| PA State Sales Tax | 6.0% of base |
| Allegheny County Tax | 2.0% of base |
| Airport Concession Recovery Fee | ~10–11% of base |
| Customer Facility Charge (CFC) | ~$5–6 / day |
| Vehicle License Recovery Fee (VLF) | ~$1.50 / day |
| **Total effective overhead** | **~25–33% on base + ~$6.50/day fixed** |

Prices from **Discovercars.com** (checked June 25, 2026) are all-inclusive of the above but **exclude** the young driver surcharge (collected at the counter).

## Hertz + AAA Deep Dive

### Why AAA Is Worth It (Especially at Age 21)

AAA Classic membership (Central Pennsylvania AAA):
- Year 1: **$84** (includes $15 one-time enrollment fee)
- Years 2+: **$69/year**

**Savings per 5-day Hertz rental:**

| Benefit | Value |
|---|---|
| Young Driver Fee waived ($19 × 5 days) | $95 |
| ~20% off base rate (est. $350 base → $70 off) | $70 |
| **Total per-trip savings** | **~$165** |

**Net savings over 4 years (1 rental per year, renting until 25):**

| Year | Age | AAA Cost | YDF Waived? | Trip Savings | Net |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 21 | $84 | ✅ | $165 | **+$81** |
| 2 | 22 | $69 | ✅ | $165 | **+$96** |
| 3 | 23 | $69 | ✅ | $165 | **+$96** |
| 4 | 24 | $69 | ✅ | $165 | **+$96** |
| **Total** | | **$291** | | **$660** | **+$369** |

At 2 Hertz rentals per year: **~+$900 net over 4 years**.

**Verdict:** Buy AAA before this Pittsburgh trip. The membership pays for itself on this single rental alone (savings ~$165 > cost $84). After age 25, reassess — the YDF waiver disappears and you'd need the other benefits (hotels, roadside, insurance discounts) to justify renewal.

### How to Book Hertz + AAA

1. Go to Hertz.com → Enter CDP code for AAA (obtain from AAA.com or your membership card)
2. Verify the rate shows the AAA/AAR discount applied to the base rate AND no YDF line item
3. Do NOT use prepaid/Slash Deal rates — AAA fee waiver does not stack with prepaid discounts
4. Bring physical AAA card to the counter

## Aggregator Sites vs. Direct Booking

- **Discovercars.com**: Most reliable all-in pricing; showed real August dates
- **AutoSlash**: Monitors for price drops and applies corporate codes automatically; worth using for any company
- **KAYAK**: Good for comparison shopping but blocks detailed scraping; prices are averages, not guaranteed
- **Priceline/Expedia**: Mystery rates may restrict age 21 rentals; verify before booking
- **Costco Travel**: Legitimate discounts (5–15%) on Budget, Avis, Alamo for Costco members; no YDF waiver
